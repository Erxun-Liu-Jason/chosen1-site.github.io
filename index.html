Cybersecurity

What does it do?

Cybersecurity means that the software and hardware of the data in the system and the network system are saved from being leaked, changed, or damaged by unexpected or vicious reasons. With a tried and continuous operation of the network service and the system can not be broken down.

Network firewall technology is a particular interconnection network equipment, which is used to enhance the access control between networks, hinder the users of the outer network from iniquitously entering the internal network through the external network, access to the internal network resources, and protect the internal network operating environment. It checks packets transmitted between two or more networks, such as link patterns, according to inevitable security policies, to decide if the telecommunications between networks is allowed or not, and invigilators the working state of the network.

At present, the firewall products mainly include fortress host, packet filter router, application layer gateway, circuit layer gateway, shielding host firewall, dual host, etc. Anti-virus software is definitely the most widely used security technology solution because the technology is the easiest to implement, but we all know is the main function of antivirus software antivirus, the function is very limited, can not fully meet the needs of network security, this way maybe can meet the needs of individuals or small businesses, but if individuals or businesses have the demand of electronic commerce, It can't be completely satisfied.

Fortunately, with the continuous development of anti-virus software technology, the current mainstream anti-virus software can prevent the Trojan horse and other hacker programs. Other antivirus software developers also provide a software firewall, with a certain firewall function, to a certain extent can play the role of a hardware firewall, such as KV300, Golden Mountain firewall, Norton firewall, and so on.

The security technology used in combination with firewall includes file encryption and digital signature technology, its purpose is to improve the security and confidentiality of information systems and data. One of the primary technical means to prevent secret data from being stolen, intercepted, or destroyed by outsiders. With the development of information technology, people be more and more concentrate on network security and information confidentiality.

At present, in accretion to enhancing data security safeguard in law and management, countries have also taken estimates in hardware and software technology respectively. It promotes the continuous improvement of data encryption skills and physical protection skills. According to the different functions, file encryption and digital signature technology basically include data transmission, data storage, data integrity discrimination, and so on.

Autonomous vehicles

What does it do?

The autonomous vehicle is a kind of intelligent vehicle, also known as a wheeled mobile robot. It mainly relies on the computer system in the car, to reach the target of driving without the driver. The autonomous vehicle is a smart vehicle that able to latches the road environment by the onboard sensing system, plans the driving guidelines by the computers, and controls the vehicle to reach the scheduled target. Like so much else, Autonomous driving is a process of gradual technological development. There are also different stages of Autonomous driving.

Stage one: the auxiliary driving stage. adaptive cruise, Lane-keeping, and other support driving capabilities are the technologies that we have at this stage, however, the navigator still is the main operator.

Stage two: semi-autonomous. At this stage, the autonomous vehicle already can project the process of going to the destination in the computer calculation, and it also could serve as a backup system to support during the driving. However, due to the restriction of regulations and laws and other factors, it still can not occur as the main part of the driving.

Stage three: Full self-driving. Technology, law, cost, and other factors cease to influence the popularity of the opportunities, computer control system has been the driving subject, the driver could take charge of the operating system anytime.

Concerning automatic parking, the cause of vehicle damage is not a recurrent road accident, but a minor bump caused by parking. Parking can be the least critical driving behavior, but it may still make everything messy. Although several automakers are adding rear-view cameras and accelerometers that scale the distance of objects around them and through onboard computers that able to display what's on the sides of the car however some drivers are still bumping their way into parking spaces.

The autonomous parking system is a major milestone of autonomous vehicles. With this system, the car is able to look into its surroundings similar to a driver, work at the right moment and travel securely from stop 1 to stop 2. While the technology isn't yet fully hands-off and letting the car drive you home, it's the first step in that direction.

Since DARPA's ALV project in the 1980s, the most popular self-driving techniques we've seen are studded with sensors and have a lidar mounted on top of them. The vehicle uses sensors to detect and three-dimensional scans of lidar to 'sense' the world around it, while the onboard controlling computer acts like a human brain to decide what to do. Machine Learning

What does it do?

Technological innovation is the fundamental driving force of economic growth. The most important thing of these technologies is what economists refer to as "general-purpose technologies" such as the internal combustion engine, the steam engine, electricity, and so on. Artificial intelligence is undoubtedly the most important "general-purpose technology" of our era. Especially the combination of artificial intelligence and industry has released the potential of the industry and change our lives.

"Machine learning" is the most important driving force behind the revolutionary advances in the field of artificial intelligence that has received so much attention.

Machine learning is an interdisciplinary subject, which involves decision theory, algorithm complexity theory, experimental science, function approximation theory, optimization theory, cybernetics, computer science, probability statistics, and other disciplines. Therefore, there are many different definitions of machine learning, each of which is defined from the perspective of related disciplines. But in general, the core issue of its concern is how to simulate human-like learning behavior computationally: to take the rules (or models) from historical experience and apply them to new and similar scenarios.

Decision trees and their variants are algorithms that divide the input area into many different regions, and each region has its independent parameters. The decision tree algorithm makes the best of the tree model, the root node to a leaf node is a separate classification of the path rules, each leaf node represents an independent judgment category. The samples are first divided into different parts, and then the segmentation recursion is executed until each subset gets samples of the same type. The prediction category can be gotten by starting the test from the root node to the subtree and then to the leaf node. This method has a simple structure and high efficiency of data processing.

An artificial neural network is similar to an unusually complex network of neurons, consisting of individual interconnected units, each of which has a numerical input and output, either in the form of a real number or a linear combination function. It has to learn in a discipline of learning before it can work. When a network makes a wrong judgment, it can learn to make it less likely to make the same mistake. This method has strong generalization ability and nonlinear mapping ability and can model the system with little information. From the point of view of functional simulation, it has parallelism, and the speed of information transmission is extremely fast.

In the process of machine learning, parameter estimation methods such as maximum likelihood estimation are needed. In the case of potential variables, the EM algorithm is usually chosen, which does not directly estimate the maximum of the function object but adds some data for simplified calculation and then carries out maximization simulation. It is a maximum likelihood estimation algorithm for data that are limited by the experience or difficult to be processed directly in the field of machine learning, deep learning is a recent research direction, which is presented into machine learning to achieve it closer to the initial target artificial intelligence (AI).

The purpose of deep learning is to study the inherent rules and representation steps of a sample target, and the information captured during the process of studying is a benefit for helping the explanation of data such as words, graphs, and voice. The final aim is for machines to be as thinking and studying as real humans, can identify those data are collected such as texts, photos, and voices. Deep learning is a sophisticated machine learning arithmetic that has far overcome antecedent technologies in voice and graph identification.

Deep learning has already achieved several efforts in search skills, natural language processing, machine learning, multimedia learning, data mining, machine translation, individuation technologies, big data analysis, and recommendation, voice identifies, and other related fields. Deep learning empowers machines to simulate human activities such as audio-visual and make choice, settles several hard pattern identification issues, and realizes significantly improvement in artificial intelligence-related technologies.

Natural language processing and chatterbots

What does it do?

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a subfield of linguistics and artificial intelligence, particularly contributed to help computer understand, process and manipulate the statements and words in human language. In other words, natural language processing is the technology that program computers to answer questions the way humans do and allow them to give appropriate response, for example Siri and Alexa. These artificial intelligences can be classified as chatterbots. Chatterbots refer to computer science which generate automated responses to human’s inputs through conversation and written words. Importantly, chatterbots are depended on natural language processing. With NLP, chatterbots can learn to read, decipher, and interpret human written and spoken language and create narratives that describe, summarize, or interpret input (structured data) in a human-like manner. NLP is the driving force behind many of the AI solutions that users frequently interact with and enables understanding between humans and machines.

So far, Natural Language Processing is booming in the best time in its history, as the technology continues to advance and integrate with various industries and implementations. The latest report showed that the market value of NLP achieved at USD 10.72 billion in 2021, predicting that it will be reached at about 48 billion in the next ten years (Natural Language Processing (NLP) Market | Growth, Trends, COVID-19 Impact, and Forecasts (2021 - 2026), 2021). As humanity enters the age of intelligence, with unprecedented growth in smart devices and data volumes, the demand for human-computer interaction through speech and language is growing exponentially, and more influential cutting-edge research and technology innovations will emerge in the field of speech and natural language processing.

Machine translate is the most fundamental application which interacted with natural language. It automatically translate text or speech from foreign language to native language. The traditional rules of process is based on the blending of language, grammar and related dictionaries. According to Devura and Jain (2020, p.3), until 2020, about a quarter of all enterprises have applied chatterbots processed by NLP into their customer services. It implies an importance and comprehensive use of chatterbots based on NLP in recent decades. Siri system is regarded as a typical representative of chatterbots. It is a dialogue system which is able to understand human language and return a response.

The future trend of natural language processing is the deep integration of NLP with many fields, thus creating value for various related industries. The need for natural language processing is increasing in fields such as banking, electrical appliances and medicine. The closer NLP is integrated with various industries, the stronger the trend of specialized services will be In the field of medical and health care, natural language processing technology has a broad application prospect, such as the auxiliary entry of medical records, medical data retrieval and analysis, and auxiliary diagnosis, etc. Modern medical information is vast, and new medical methods and approaches are developing rapidly. These factors all contribute to the difficulties in NLP implementation. You must have access to large amounts of natural language data so that computers can interact extensively. There are many scenarios in which NLP can be used. It enables machines to perform repetitive tasks that would otherwise be performed by humans, thus helping to scale up language-related tasks. With the explosive development of e-commerce, NLP plays an important role in the search result query. It helps to identify key words in order to acquire relevant information. Basically, when users entered products’ details into search box in Amazon, it fruitfully targets the exact products which save much time shopping for things outside. Additionally, NLP enables AI to communicate with people about everyday problems and matters, freeing up more time for humans to do higher-level strategic work. Based on the application scenarios of chatbots, there are 3 main types of chatbots. Question-answer system is usually used in home appliance control in smart home which makes human life more convenient and safer. A Dialogue System is usually oriented towards a task where the robot needs to interact with the user, even for multiple rounds. Finally, business resources need to be invoked to complete a task. For example, a bot that checks the weather. Casual chat is a kind of open domain chatting scenario, just like chatting between two friends without restricting the topic and content. Chatty bots are mainly used for personal assistants, entertainment and other scenarios, with typical representatives such as Microsoft Ice and Apple Siri.

Project Ideas

Problem needs to be solved

In present days, the main problem has become to have access to food during the pandemic of Covid 19. At this point, people are struggling between the second wave of COVID-19 where citizens are being more cautious about stepping out of their house. While people are maintaining social distancing by a large extent, there are certain cases where people are needed to go outside to acquire food. At that point of time, they can be in contact with this virus. In this case, the global economy is deteriorating significantly and restaurant industry in the country of Australia is facing a great loss. Therefore it is the responsibility of Australians to save its economy. In this proper solution to this problem is needed.

Overview

In order to recover from this situation, smartphone applications for food ordering can be developed through which the restaurant can update its traditional menu ordering system with a smart menu ordering system (Cha and Seo, 2020). In this manner, the users or the guests who are coming to the restaurant do not have to touch their mem card which can contain COVID virus. At this point, this application can assist the restaurants to embrace zero-contact service to people of Australia for combatting this deadly pandemic.

Motivation

It is fortunate that Australia country is one the verge of being in a better position, whereas the other countries are facing the second wave. At this point, people of this country still need to take precautions carefully in order to avoid the extensive spread of this pandemic. At this point, digital innovation is that particular aspect which can assist people in maintaining social distance from each other. In this regard, digital menu ordering systems can help them in adhering to co contact principles throughout the pandemic. In addition to this, people can take control of their activities while going out to any restaurants.

Target market

After severe consequences of COVID-19, people have started going out again. In this case, the main target market for this application is mostly people of Australia who are eager to eat any restaurant while following precautionary measures for obstructing the spread of COVID-19. Under the circumstances, place demographic attributes can be considered as the main target since people of any age and employment status or any other aspects can come to restaurants. In addition to this, the other customer group can be identified as a target market who have a smartphone. Since the entire project revolves around smartphones, so people of Australia with smartphones can be a greater target market for this application.

Technical specification

Hardware requirements

There are external hardware devices can be utilized in this proposed application. Two mobile phones and a computer must be used for the proceeding of this application related to the restaurant menu ordering system. Each of the devices should be immune to COVID-19 spread and proper caution must be used while operating these devices. The smartphones will be used for two end user requirements (core.ac.uk, 2021). The first one can be used by the users or the guests who will be coming to the restaurants. And, the other one will be used from the restaurant side in order to take orders and other attributes. In this case, computer system will be used as one back end server which can store the application databases and back-end program of the application. Therefore, it is mandatory that the information will be stored only in the computer server not in those two smartphones.

Software requirements

For designing the interface of this ordering application, MIT App Inventor can be used. Certain development languages are required for designing the background program for this application. In this case, python or java can be effective. APII interface design along with database applications, database software libraries such as SQLite must be utilized for backend programming of this menu ordering application. Python can be used for managing databases including storing user information (Bhargave et al., 2013). For this advanced application, user information can include user details, login credentials, restaurant menus and so on. Furthermore, encryption mechanisms must be used for encrypting personal information of users along with password encryption. In this regard, RSA algorithm can be used for the effectiveness of this application.

Skill requirements

Extensive knowledge about programming languages such as python or java must be acquired for developing source code for this menu ordering application for restaurants. Furthermore, several aspects of API and database management system plus query language must be known for proper development of this application. In case of API, the functions of an API such as Google Map API must be known for designing this independently. Database management system knowledge can help in carrying out activities related to database engines such as SQLite. Encryption technologies should also be covered for encrypting personal user information for this application.

Description

User application

Initially user registration or user login page must be developed so that users can have access to their individual private account for the restaurants. For this, user information like user name, address, phone number must be put into the system. Although these private information are collected, the data will be stored in a database server through encryption technology for security purposes. If the user is frequent in any restaurant then the user preference will also be stored along with recommended menu items making it easy for the users to order (Patil et al., 2017). After selecting the restaurant they have gone to, they can see dinning time, number of people who are dining in so that they can make a choice of whether they desire to eat at that restaurant or not. Customer rating facilities must be added in this application for other customers to know about the protocols of restaurants. Since, there can be several restaurants, a particular sort index must be added which can show a range of restaurants based on service rank, business hours, score and current distance. In case the users need assistance during their meal they can also call waiter from the waiter service function designed for providing real time communication facilities.

Restaurant side application

From restaurant side application, the login interface must be designed for the restaurants. Logging into their personal accounts, restaurants can edit its contact number, address, number of people currently dining, maximum number of people, business hours, menu orders and additional information (iaetsdjaras, 2021). Through Google Map API the restaurants can add their address and directions to get their. Additionally, restaurants can display its introduction along with its signature dishes, the number of customers who are in line and the hygiene protocols followed by the restaurants for the customers who are inclined towards avoiding contact with anyone. Menu options can present options based on different filters and sorting procedures such as lower price first or higher price first. Furthermore for frequent customers their previously ordered items can additionally be present there. Dishes ordering based on different flavors can also be generated for accessibility of customers.

Payment application

Online payment application modules can reduce human touch to a large extent because of cashless transactions. In payment application, relevant API functionalities of banks must be obtained. At this point, bank details of the users must be collected for initiating transactions (Li et al., 2020). After completing the transaction from the bank side, an automated bill must be generated. This bill information will be stored by both customer end and restaurant end.

Outcome

This developed smartphone restaurant menu ordering system can improve the traditional ordering system which is prone to human contact because of high dependence upon paper based processes (Munene and Kasamani, 2018). The concerned solution can assist the customers in ordering their foods at any restaurants at their basic comfort while reducing human contact due to transaction bills, menu cards and other aspects. Furthermore the customers can be able to know about restaurants’ current business timing, customer gatherings in order to avoid larger gatherings which can spread COVID 19. The implementation technologies such as DBMS, SQLite, Python, Java and API can provide technical advancement to menu ordering systems which can follow the basic rule of COVID19 precaution such as social distancing. Therefore, it can be conjectured that this application can provide customers who are particularly concerned about their restaurant choices during this pandemic. Therefore the safety ratings, business conditions and other attributes can assist customers in making suitable restaurant choices.

Feasibility of project

This menu ordering application can attest to development of current customer trends towards, real-time and convenient technological innovation. In this case, the menu ordering system through smartphones can substitute the utilization of paper which is inconvenient and critical in present COVID 19 circumstances (Aulia et al., 2017). Through this application, both customers and restaurants can be benefited in their respective context such as dining in a safe restaurant and improving business productivity.
